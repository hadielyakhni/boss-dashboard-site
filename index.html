Why Is It Special

Easy to use

When building this application, we took into consideration enabling the user to navigate and use all the features in an easy and simple way.

All in one

Single app to manage your tasks, access your employees and check your money transactions.

Offline - Online usage

You can use Boss Dashboard offline, and all your changes will be synced with your account when you got back online, allowing you to access your account data from many devices.

More details about the app!

Boss Dashboard help you manage your business by accessing your employees and checking your transactions easily.

Also, Boss Dashboard offers a simple todo list to register and track all your tasks!

available on Google Store

Other Features

Fast Signup-Login process

Create an account using facebook, google or simply your email-address and enjoy accessing your data from multi devices.

High customizable

Boss Dashboard provides dark and light theme, and also provides 3 languages to choose between: English, French and Arabic.

Realtime updates

Access your data from any device, as all of your data will be synced between devices when getting online.

Data sorting

Sort your tasks, employees and wallet data in any order you want to get the most out of these data.
